The Prison Healer

By: Lynette Noni

The Prison Healer follows Kiva Meridan, who works at the notorious death prison Zalindov as it's only qualified healer. She has managed to survive there for 10 years by only focusing on one goal: stay alive. But then the Rebel Queen arrives at the prison, deathly ill, and Kiva receives a message:'dont let her die.

Book Summary

Kiva decides she has to save the Rebel Queen even if it means she'll die. There are also many rebel groups within the prison that are ensuring that Kiva will save the Queen. She also becomes close with Jaren and begins to want to protect him as well. She takes the Queens spot in the trials to save the queen but many believe she signed a death wish. However, if she makes it through the trials she can leave the prison and be free. As she goes through the trials something magical keeps saving her. At the end there is major plot twist that reveals who the characters really are.

Lynette Noni is an Australian author who studied journalism, academic writing, and human behavior at university before venturing into the world of fiction.

She is now a full-time writer and the #1 bestselling and award-winning author of The Medoran Chronicles, the Whisper duology, The Prison Healer, The Gilded Cage, and The Blood Traitor.

Why I Like This Book

I very much enjoyed this book because it has excitement, fantasy, and romance. There are so many plot twists once I though I had something figured out it reveals a whole new plot. At the end of the book it left me with the biggest cliffhanger and huge plot twist making me want to read the next book immediately.
